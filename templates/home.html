ABOUT US:-

MicroSolutionIndia is the leading A+, N+, MCSE, MCTS, MCITP, CCNA & ORACLE Training Institute of New Delhi, India. It is managed by a team of highly talented professionals who have more than 30 years of combined I.T. and Corporate Training experience. The sole aim of Institute of MicroSolutionIndia is to fill the ever increasing gap between the skills required in Industry and possessed by the prospective employees (engineering and MCA students). The goal of Institute of MicroSolutionIndia to become top most IT Training Institute of the country in the years to come. To achieve our goal we have charted a path for ourselves, main elements of which are our students and professionals who shall move us to the top. The success that we have achieved so far is the indicator that we are moving swiftly towards our goal.
